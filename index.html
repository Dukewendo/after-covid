Falconry experience

Want to experience something together you’ll never forget? Head to Millets Falconry Centre for this private Falconry Experience for Two. You’ll gain an understanding of these incredible creatures, and with the guidance of an experienced Falconer, you’ll get to handle and fly between three and five different birds of prey. You’ll learn about their lives in the wild and in captivity, and once your experience has finished, you can enjoy all-day entry to the rest of the centre, where you can discover more exotic species. An amazing experience for anyone interested in birds of prey, or those who simply fancy trying something different.

Flying experience


Feel the thrill of piloting a light aircraft then sit back and observe as somebody else takes the controls in this Land Away Double Flying Lesson.
You’ll have the chance to experience two kinds of flying lesson in this unique adventure that allows you to act as pilot on one leg of the journey and as an observer on the second leg. You’ll land and take off from different locations, subject to the airfield and weather conditions, taking it in turns with one other person to pilot the plane, and any time spent behind the controls will count towards a Private Pilot’s licence. Once you have completed both flight legs, you will be presented with a certificate confirming completion – the perfect memento for an extraordinary experience.

Paintballing experience


Have you tried paintballing? This combat sport requires teamwork and skill to defeat your opposition. It’s an adrenaline filled game of stealth and strategy where the objective is to eliminate the enemy. It will feel like real warfare as you work together to hunt them down and take them out before they can do the same to you. Paintballs will fly and you will have to run, dodge and hide in the undergrowth to escape. Play well and they will never spot you. If you get hit, you’re out, but never fear there will be up to ten scenarios, which reset each time, for you to test your aim and skill as you start a new battle. Paintballing requires you to use all your senses and keep your nerves in check; a steady hand hits the target. Will you go it alone like Rambo or work with your friends to ensure victory for you all?

Clay Shooting experience with refreshments


Whether you are a seasoned professional or a complete novice, the fantastic outdoor pursuit of clay shooting will either fuel your passion, or be the start of a brand new hobby for the two of you. Your experience will include all equipment hire, clays and cartridges as well as expert tuition. You will both be given all the necessary training in safety techniques from a fully qualified instructor. The professionals are friendly and patient and will teach you how to handle the shotgun and shoot a variety of sporting targets, so you’ll be well prepared when you step up to have a go for yourselves, putting your aim and accuracy to the test. Choose from 12 venues across the UK and have an hour’s shooting with the clays. You’ll also enjoy seasonal refreshments.

Tank driving experience

Take to the wheel of some mean military machinery and test your skills and your nerves on a specially designed course, designed to give you the military driving experience of a lifetime. Following a safety briefing and tuition, you and your crew will take it in turns to handle the controls as you drive through, over and across the range of thrilling mud and water obstacles in your path. With a choice of locations, the vehicles you drive will differ depending on where you choose, but you can be assured of a driving experience unlike any other.

Zombie escape experience

Step into the shoes of a horror movie character with this Zombie Apocalypse Experience in Greater Manchester or West Yorkshire. There are two stories to get involved with here, either at ground zero of the zombie outbreak at The Labs or a few months after an escape of the undead to The Mill nearby. Whichever event you choose you’ll be in for 2 hours 30 minutes of terrifying action. Be warned, this is a full hands-on experience, so if you’re not keeping an eye out you might get dragged off into the darkness by scores of hungry zombies. You’ll be fully trained in all the necessary weaponry and tactics, including a shotgun that can fire projectiles at over 350 feet per second. The only thing that stands between zombies and the end of the world is you.

Zorbing experience

Have you tried zorbing? It’s the funniest way to get down a hill, inside a giant inflatable ball designed to turn your world upside down. You’ll hurtle down a purpose-built run at speeds of over 20mph. Harness Zorbing is the conventional way to ride one of these balls, which have been specially designed to create a comfortable and supportive environment for your ride. Soft PVC pads and harnessing ensure you are protected from any lumps and bumps. But don't get too comfortable - once you start the rolling you’ll be in for the ride of your life. Fancy something more extreme? Choose Hydro Zorbing. Forget the harness and add 40 litres of water into your zorb and you’ve got yourself a wild water ride for two. Ever wondered what it’s like inside a washing machine? Hydro Zorbing is the closest you’ll ever come to finding out. Take your pick!

You’ll get to drive a total of up to 15 miles in your chosen cars, so you’ll have plenty of time to get the adrenaline flowing and feel every thrilling twist and turn! As part of your day, strap in for a High Speed Passenger Ride from a professional driver. To finish, you’ll receive a photo of yourself speeding around the track in a magnificent supercar, the perfect keepsake to remember your extraordinary experience.
